# TCLCubicMeter

### Volume

$\overline V_{123} = \displaystyle\overline A_{12} ∧ \overline x_3$ , 

where 

- $\overline V_{123}$ is the volume-trivector on $e_{123}$ space, $[m^3]$;
- $\overline A_{12}$ is area-bivector on the $e_{12}$ plane, $[m^2]$;
- $\overline x_{3}$ is length-vector along the $e_{3}$ axis, $[m]$;

and

- $\overline A_{12} = \displaystyle\frac{\overline V_{123} ⋅ \overline x_3}{ |\overline x_3|^2}$ ;

- $\overline x_3 = \displaystyle\frac{\overline A_{12} ⋅ \overline V_{123}}{ |\overline A_{12}|^2}$ .
